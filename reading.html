What I'm reading

  In 2026, I realized that since I’m a researcher and will have to travel a lot and live wherever work takes me, I wouldn’t be able to build up a book collection. So I decided to at least take notes on the books I’ve read and post them on my website. I add a book to the list if I found it interesting in some way, so it doesn’t necessarily mean I liked it.
