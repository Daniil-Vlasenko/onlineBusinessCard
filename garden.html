So will the garden wither?

The man had a garden and two sons. He called the first one and said:
- Take care of the garden.
And he answered:
- Yes, sure Father. I love this garden and will be happy to take care of it.
But after the conversation with his father, he became lazy and decided not to take care of the garden. The father called the second son and said:
- Take care of the garden.
In response:
- I won't do it for anything, it's disgusting and I'm not interested in it.
After the conversation, the son thought that it would still be a pity if the garden withered, and reluctantly began to take care of it.

So will the garden wither?
