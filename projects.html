Automated Instagram account - Contrart'o.

The automated Instagram account independently creates media content and writes text to posts based on databases obtained from Sotheby's and Christie's auction sites, as well as implements a self-promotion algorithm.
Account subject. There is no fair price in the art market, because, firstly, the supply is limited, there are no two identical works, and, secondly, the demand is extremely subjective. Thus, the account tries to raise a philosophical problem - is it possible to evaluate art fairly? Each post is a comparison of one of the cheapest and one of the most expensive paintings by any author, with the author's name, the names of the paintings, the dates of their sale, their prices and art critics' ratings.
